I believe we've passed an age where websites are just containers for information. People don't stay on sites anymore, they skim and leave. I wanted to build something different: a portfolio that tells a story using imagination, color, and interaction. My World Site isn't just a resume, it's an immersive experience that captures who I am and where I come from. 

The site is built on layers of interaction, but the clock is the true threshold. Clicking it transports you into the dream world inside my head—a kingdom where I weave my stories. Here, the castle stands as a symbol of security and authority over the reality I create, reflecting the autonomy of my imagination—how I live and how I yearn to live. The path leading to the house represents the journey home to Guatemala, while the surrounding flora evokes the vibrant landscapes of Latin America. It's personal geography translated into digital space. (I eventually plan to turn the castle sequence into a full-on comic book.) All built in HTML, CSS, JS
